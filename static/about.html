About 3D Print Architecture

Covering architectural additive manufacturing at every scale — from the desktop model that wins a competition to the robotic extrusion that builds the wall.

OUR STORY

Architecture at the Frontier of Fabrication

3D Print Architecture covers the full spectrum of architectural additive manufacturing — from the desktop model that wins a competition to the robotic concrete extrusion that builds the wall.

Our editorial team includes architects, fabrication engineers, and computational designers with real project experience across scale models, concrete construction, facade panel manufacturing, and parametric design-to-fabrication workflows.

PURPOSE

Our Mission

To cover architectural AM from 1:500 concept models to full-scale concrete structures with equal depth. The same design logic applies at every scale — and we understand both the computation and the concrete behind it.

Our Vision

To be the authoritative, vendor-independent source for construction and architectural 3D printing — connecting architects, builders, and engineers to the suppliers and technology reshaping how buildings get made.
